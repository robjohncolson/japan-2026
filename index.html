Logistics & reference

Rental car

  • Vehicle: 9-night Kumamoto base for expressway + mountain day-trips (Aso ~1.5h, Kurokawa ~2h, Takachiho ~2.5h) plus Hakata runs. Prefer a kei car (N-Box / Tanto / Hustler) or a compact (Fit / Yaris / Note / Swift) over a kei truck.
  • Request: automatic, non-smoking, ETC unit + rental ETC card, English nav, CDW and NOC waiver — confirm both.
  • KEP (Kyushu Expressway Pass): max ~10 days, can't cover the full ~22-day rental; Times / ORIX / Nissan / Nippon accept it (not Toyota/Budget).
  • Drives: Hakata→Kanzaki ~45–60 min · Hakata→Kashima ~1h · Kanzaki↔Kashima ~30–45 min.

Mobile — two SIMs

  • Sakura Mobile (NTT Docomo) — English-friendly; order before the trip, deliver to do-c or pick up at Narita.
  • au / KDDI — second line for rural reach; prepaid SIM from Yodobashi Akiba 1F or the airport.

Bicycle — Tokyo

  • How: Docomo Bike Share or Hello Cycling apps (e-assist, ~¥1,000–2,000/day, docks at Ebisu & across Shibuya, in-app pay, built-in lock, no helmet), or a full-day bike from CycleTrip (Akihabara) or a local Ebisu shop.
  • Front (29 Jul): short shakedown around Ebisu. Tail (25 Aug): Yamanote loop (~35 km), dawn start, 33–36°C. (26 Aug): Yokohama Chinatown / waterfront ride.
  • Rules: from 1 Apr 2026, cyclists 16+ get "blue-ticket" fines — no phone while riding, obey signals, lights at night.
