    • 23 Jul: bed midnight · wake 8:00 a.m.
    • 24 Jul: bed 11:30 p.m. · wake 7:30 a.m.
    • 25–26 Jul: bed ~11:00 p.m. · wake ~7:00 a.m.

    JL007 flight sleep (watch → Tokyo at gate)

    • Takeoff: 27 Jul 13:15 EDT = Tokyo 02:15 a.m. JST 28 Jul · land 4:00 p.m. JST · ~13h45 in the air.
    • ASAP after sit (Tokyo still night): socks on · Trtl · bathroom · Mantis if bright · sleep as soon as you can — do not stay up “a few hours first.”
    • Main sleep: aim ~5–8 h from takeoff. 8 h ≈ wake ~10 a.m. Tokyo — perfect. Trtl + blackout mask + plugs · cypro (usual dose) at start of block if using.
    • After wake → landing (~5 h if you slept 8 h): AWAKE — Mantis off · coffee OK · laptop / games / eat / drink · walk aisle. Fill the time on purpose.
    • Land 4 p.m. → first night: stay up (Sakura · do-c · Moti OK). Mantis from ~9 p.m. · Mg threonate + cypro · target sleep ~11 p.m. · wake 7–8 a.m. (not 1 a.m.). No long nap after landing.

    Sleep aids

    • Cyproheptadine: preferred over melatonin (Peat-ish). Established dose only — pre-shift nights + start of plane sleep block. No progesterone · no melatonin unless cypro gone.
    • Flight kit (packed): Trtl Pillow Plus (height-adjustable neck) · compression socks 20–30 mmHg · 100% blackout eye mask · earplugs/ANC · Mantis blue blockers (evenings / pre-sleep only) · water · socks on before takeoff.

    30-day pack — hard to get only

    • Daily core: Mg threonate at night · TTFD (thiamine) in the morning · aspirin (usual low dose; flight + trip).
    • D3 + K2: yes, bring — indoor/capsule days + summer sun is unreliable; hard to match your dose from konbini food.
    • Zinc: only ~10 doses (every ~3 days). Japan = seafood/oysters when you want; don’t airlift a full month.
    • Vitamin C: don’t pack — hunt mikan / oranges / kiwi / peppers (last year tangerine run is fine and on-theme).
    • Vitamin E: optional thin stack if you already use it daily; else skip to save space.
    • Skip: progesterone · melatonin · boron · vitamin A pills · full zinc bottle · anything not habitual.

    Rule: pack what Japan won’t hand you (Mg threonate, TTFD, D+K, aspirin, a few zinc). Eat the rest. Original bottles · carry-on for the flight half. Not medical advice.
